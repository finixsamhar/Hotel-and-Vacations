Application name: Hotel and Vacations
Purpose: Allowing users to manage and organize their vacations and excursions, by typing and saving their desired vacations along with their specific excursion, 
selecting their desired dates, updating their records, and delete them whenever they want.

Directions:
This application allows the user to enter, update and delete vacations, it consistently stores and retrieves submitted vacations and implements validation that prevents a vacation from being deleted if an excursion is associated.
A user can click the "Visit" button in the first page, then choose even to create his/her vacation from the menu located on the top right "Add new vacation" or in the bottom of the screen on the right. Next, he/she can type and fill up the vacation title, hotel name, start, and end date.
Then he/she can click th menu in the top right corner to save or delete that vacation.
Users may use th left arrow to see their vacations list if they already saved them, then decide to delete them or add excursions to them.
if users added excursions and they decided to click delete vacation it will not be allowed in this case because the vacation selected is associated with at least one excursion.
Users are allowed to delete vacations as long as those vacations are independent from any excursion. They are also allowed to update existing vacation by clicking on vacation in th "vacation list", clicking on menu "vacation details and excursion lists", then choosing update vacation, they will see the cursor focusing on the title of their existing vacation.
Users can safely enter their preferred start and end date for their vacation by just clicking on the dates field, a calendar will pop up with no need for typing date or worrying about making errors in dates.
In addition to that, the application is smart enough to detect the start date that must be before de end date, so a message will pop up and notify users of that possible error.
Users can also get notified whenever they go to vacation details specifically clicking in the menu at the top right and choose notify, this option will help them remember the start and the end date of their vacation.
They may share their vacation details too, by clicking on share vacation exists in the same menu.
One the other hand, if users choose to add an excursion for a certain vacation, they can go to the bottom of the vacation details, on the right, they click on the floating action button so that it will take them to the excursion page or screen, on top right there is a menu where they can add, update, or delete their excursion
Once they save an excursion, it would automatically be associated with its chosen vacation, if they want to delete that vacation, they need to delete its excursion first.
The option of sharing and getting notified about the excursion is also available in the excursion details menu. The excursion details include name or title of the excursion, date, hotel as an optional choice, and also a note.
Finally, all the requirements of task B5 are existed and implemented correctly.
